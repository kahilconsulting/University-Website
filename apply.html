What happens next

  1. Pay the A$75 application fee via the secure link in your confirmation email. Assessment begins once payment is received.
  2. We verify your documents. Our admissions team reviews your academic records and supporting documents — we'll contact you if anything further is needed.
  3. Receive a decision. Most domestic applicants hear back within 10 business days; international applications take 2–4 weeks.
  4. Accept your offer through the applicant portal to secure your place and begin enrolment.
