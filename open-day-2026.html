Schedule

What to Expect on the Day

A full day of activities to help you make your study decision with confidence.

🕙

9:00 AM — Doors Open

Arrive from 9:00 AM. Collect your welcome pack and personalised schedule from the main registration marquee at the University Drive entrance.

🎤

9:30 AM — Welcome Address

Vice-Chancellor's welcome in the Great Hall, followed by a panel of current students sharing their Tarelium experience.

🏛️

10:00 AM — Campus Tours

Guided tours depart every 20 minutes. Choose from a general campus tour or faculty-specific tours covering labs, studios, and specialised facilities.

📚

10:30 AM – 1:00 PM — Faculty Sessions

Parallel information sessions across all faculties. Speak with academics, get program details, entry requirements, and career outcomes.

🍽️

1:00 PM — Lunch Break

Complimentary lunch served on the Central Lawn. Live music, student club stalls, and informal networking with current students.

🏅

1:30 PM – 3:00 PM — Scholarships & Wrap-Up

Scholarship Q&A session, final campus tours, and one-on-one appointments with admissions advisors. Doors close at 3:00 PM.

Can't Make It on the Day?

Explore our programs online, book a virtual campus tour, or contact our Admissions team for a personalised conversation about your study options.
