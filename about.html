About StudentFlow University

Founded on the belief that education transforms lives — and that every student deserves to reach their highest potential.

Our Mission

Empowering Minds, Shaping Futures

StudentFlow University exists to inspire and challenge students to develop the knowledge, skills, and character needed to thrive in a complex, fast-changing world. We are guided by a commitment to academic excellence, inclusive community, and purposeful research that makes a real difference.

Our graduates go on to lead in boardrooms, laboratories, courtrooms, clinics, and classrooms around the globe — proof that what begins here echoes far beyond our campus.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous, evidence-based teaching that raises the standard in every field we touch.

Inclusivity

A campus where every background, identity, and perspective is welcomed and valued.

Innovation

We cultivate curiosity and reward bold thinking — in classrooms and research labs alike.

Community

Students, staff, alumni, and partners working together as a single interconnected community.

SFU at a Glance

Established in 1978 on a single campus in Sydney's CBD, StudentFlow University has grown into a multi-campus institution serving students from over 90 countries. We are home to 6 faculties, 14 research centres, and one of the most active student unions in the country.

Our History

47 Years of Excellence

1978

Foundation

StudentFlow University established in Sydney with 3 faculties and 450 founding students. The first Vice-Chancellor was Professor Alan Burke.

1990

Faculty of Engineering Opens

The Faculty of Engineering & Technology opened, marking the start of our STEM focus and attracting federal research funding.

2002

International Expansion

First student exchange agreements signed with universities in the UK, USA, and Singapore. International enrolments doubled within 3 years.

2010

Research Excellence Recognition

Achieved ERA (Excellence in Research for Australia) ranking of 5 — "well above world standard" — in 7 disciplines.

2018

Innovation Hub Launch

The SFU Innovation Hub opened, housing 40+ student startups and partner incubators. Over $30M in spinout value to date.

2025

Today

12,400+ students across 6 faculties, 120+ programs, and a $120M research portfolio. The journey continues.
